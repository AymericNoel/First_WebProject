  1. Build Ourself


    2. Studying at ESILV means choosing a generalist engineering school focused on digital technology, new technologies, hybridization and innovation. It also means being involved in your own training. There are many opportunities to compose and enrich their course: 9 majors, 21 double degrees including that of Engineer-Manager with the EMLV, 13 months minimum of internships in companies... Throughout their course, students work on projects that reinforce and structure their learning and call on their abilities as future engineers: Imagination and eXploration Project, Generalist Digital Engineer Project, Industrial Innovation Project, company challenges...

  2. Opening up to the world


    3. Exchange programmes related to the majors taught at ESILV with more than 100 partner universities in 43 countries, 6 months of academic exchange or internship abroad in the third year, 13 double degrees (Master, DESS, MSc) abroad in the final year, a track 100% in English in the fourth year... ESILV bets on international openness to nurture the personal and professional project of its students and train engineers with a multicultural sensitivity and multiple experiences abroad
