Welcome to my new project : The pi carre explanation






First of all, the dollar cost averaging is an investment with the goal of reducing the impact of volatility large purchases of financial assets such as equities.

By dividing the total sum to be invested in the market (e.g. $100,000) into equal amounts put into the market at regular intervals (e.g. $1000 over 100 weeks), DCA hopes to reduce the risk of incurring a substantial loss resulting from investing the entire "lump sum" just before a fall in the market.
